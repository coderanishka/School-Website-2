The Lodha World School, LSG sports program is undoubtedly every child’s dream come true.

On Tuesdays and Fridays, 200 hundred odd gleeful faces march out of school into the Palava Olympic Sports Complex taking a well-deserved mid-morning break from classrooms, books and teachers. The Palava Olympic sports complex brings with it a variety of facilities for sports like Swimming, Cricket, Football, Squash, Tennis and Badminton.

The Lodha World School offers swimming, badminton, football and lawn tennis to its students as a compulsory sports program integrated into the curriculum as we truly understand the benefits of sport especially for children. What makes this sports program even more special is the coaching it offers through its affiliation with one of the country’s best sports and fitness companies – Natekar Sports and Fitness. NSF brings within its fold, world class partnerships with coaching academies such as the Nisha Millet Swimming Academy, The Prakash Padukone Badminton Academy, South United Football Club and The Pinnacle Tennis Academy.
